<p><p>The triple offset valves market comprises four major types: manual, electric, pneumatic, and hydraulic. Manual type triple offset valves are operated manually by turning a handwheel or lever. Electric type valves are automated and controlled by an electric actuator. Pneumatic type valves use compressed air to operate the valve, and hydraulic type valves use hydraulic fluid pressure to control the movement. These different types cater to a wide range of applications, offering flexibility and efficient flow control in various industrial processes.</p></p>
