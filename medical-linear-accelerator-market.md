<p><p>The Medical Linear Accelerator Market consists of two major types: Low-Energy and High-Energy. Low-Energy machines utilize less power and are commonly used for superficial and orthovoltage treatments. They deliver lower radiation doses to patients and are suitable for treating shallow cancers that are close to the skin. On the other hand, High-Energy machines produce higher energy beams, enabling them to penetrate deeper into tissues to target tumors located in challenging areas of the body. These machines are typically used for treating tumors at varying depths throughout the body.</p></p>
