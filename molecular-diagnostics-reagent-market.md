<p><p>The molecular diagnostics reagent market consists of various types of kits used for different purposes. Sample preparation kits are utilized to extract and purify DNA/RNA samples. PCR assay kits help in amplifying and detecting specific DNA/RNA sequences. In situ hybridization kits identify specific nucleotide sequences in cells. Microarray kits enable the simultaneous detection of multiple DNA/RNA targets. Sequencing kits aid in determining the order of nucleotides in a DNA/RNA sample. These kits play a crucial role in molecular diagnostics by facilitating accurate and efficient analysis of genetic material.</p></p>
