<p><p>The Camera Extension Tubes market can be categorized into two types: Automatic and Manual. Automatic extension tubes are designed to automatically maintain electronic communication between the camera body and lens, allowing for autofocus and automatic aperture control. On the other hand, manual extension tubes lack electronic connections and require manual adjustment of the lens for focusing and aperture control. Both types offer photographers the ability to extend the distance between the camera and lens, enabling the capture of close-up shots or macro photography with varying degrees of convenience and control.</p></p>
