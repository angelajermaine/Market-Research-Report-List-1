<p><p>Continuous Compaction Control Systems market consists of two main types: Single Roller and Double Roller systems. Single Roller systems involve a single heavy roller used for compaction on construction sites. Double Roller systems, on the other hand, involve two heavy rollers working in tandem to compact soil or asphalt effectively. Both types of systems are used for improving compaction quality and efficiency in construction projects. Customers can choose between Single Roller and Double Roller systems based on their specific project requirements and budget constraints.</p></p>
