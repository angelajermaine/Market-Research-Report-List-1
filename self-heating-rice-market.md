<p><p>The self-heating rice market comprises two types: normal temperature and refrigeration. Normal temperature self-heating rice is designed to be stored at room temperature and does not require refrigeration. It contains a specially designed heating element that activates when water is added, heating up the rice without the need for additional heat sources. On the other hand, refrigeration self-heating rice is specifically designed to be kept in a refrigerated environment. It contains a similar heating element but requires preheating before consumption. Both types offer convenience and quick heating options for rice lovers on the go.</p></p>
