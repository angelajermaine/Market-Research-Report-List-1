<p><p>A fiber optic gyrocompass is a device used for navigation that employs the principles of fiber optic technology to measure the orientation or direction of an object. There are two main types of fiber optic gyrocompass: single-axis and multi-axis. The single-axis fiber optic gyrocompass measures the angular velocity around a single axis, providing information about the object's heading. On the other hand, the multi-axis fiber optic gyrocompass measures angular velocity around multiple axes, allowing for precise determination of the object's orientation in three-dimensional space.</p></p>
