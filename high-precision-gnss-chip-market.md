<p><p>The High Precision GNSS Chip market offers two types of receivers: Dual Receiver and Multiple Receiver. Dual Receiver systems utilize two separate GNSS receivers, enhancing accuracy by comparing the signals received from both receivers. This allows for error detection and elimination, ensuring higher precision positioning. On the other hand, Multiple Receiver systems utilize more than two GNSS receivers to further improve accuracy and reliability by combining the data from multiple sources. Both types of receivers offer increased precision in GNSS positioning for various applications.</p></p>
