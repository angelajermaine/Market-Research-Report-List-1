<p><p>The photo booth market can be divided into two main types: contactless and contact. Contactless photo booths provide a touch-free experience, where users can take photos and make selections without physically touching the equipment. These booths often utilize advanced technology such as gesture recognition or virtual props. On the other hand, contact photo booths require users to physically interact with the equipment, such as pressing buttons or using touch screens. Both types offer unique experiences and cater to different customer preferences in capturing memorable moments.</p></p>
