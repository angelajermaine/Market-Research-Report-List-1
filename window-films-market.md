The market growth can be attributed to several factors. Firstly, the increasing awareness regarding energy efficiency and sustainability has driven the demand for window films as they help in reducing energy consumption by maintaining optimal indoor temperatures. Furthermore, rising concerns about the harmful effects of UV radiation on human health and fading of furniture and flooring have boosted the adoption of window films for UV protection.</p><p>In terms of trends, the market is witnessing a shift towards advanced nanotechnology-based window films that offer enhanced performance attributes. These films use nano-scale particles to provide superior heat rejection, increased scratch resistance, and better optical clarity.
