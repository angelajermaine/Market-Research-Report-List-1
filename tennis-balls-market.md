<p><p>The tennis ball market can be categorized into three types: high pressure ball, low pressure ball, and pressureless ball. High pressure balls are pressurized with air and have a hollow core, offering high bounce and quick speed on the court. Low pressure balls have less air pressure, resulting in a slower game, making them suitable for beginners or recreational players. Pressureless balls, on the other hand, do not have an internal pressure, providing consistent bounce and durability, ideal for practice or training sessions. These different market types cater to players with distinct playing preferences and skill levels.</p></p>
