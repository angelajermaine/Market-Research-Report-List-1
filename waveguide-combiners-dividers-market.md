<p><strong>The Waveguide Combiners & Dividers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>3dB Waveguide Combiner/Divider</li><li>3dB Hybrid Waveguide Combiner/Divider</li><li>Variable Power Divider</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Waveguide combiners and dividers are devices used in microwave communication systems to combine or divide the signals within waveguides. The 3dB waveguide combiner/divider equally splits the input power between two output ports with a fixed power ratio. The 3dB hybrid waveguide combiner/divider also equally splits the input power, but it allows for adjustable power ratios. The variable power divider, on the other hand, enables non-equal power division between the output ports, allowing flexibility in power distribution. These devices are essential for efficient signal management in microwave systems.</p></p>
