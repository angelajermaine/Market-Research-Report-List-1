<p><p>Breast lumpectomy treatment, also known as breast-conserving surgery or partial mastectomy, is a surgical procedure performed to remove the tumor along with a surrounding margin of normal breast tissue. This surgical option is considered for early-stage breast cancers where the tumor is small and localized. The aim of the treatment is to remove the cancerous tissue while preserving the shape and appearance of the breast.</p><p>The breast lumpectomy treatment market is expected to experience significant growth during the forecast period. Factors driving this growth include the rising prevalence of breast cancer, increasing awareness about early detection and treatment, advancements in surgical techniques, and growing emphasis on aesthetic outcomes.
