<p><p>The Cardiac Care Medical Equipment Market encompasses various types of medical devices used for cardiac care. Cardiac ultrasound devices provide imaging of the heart's structure and function. CRM Devices are used for cardiac rhythm management, including pacemakers and implantable defibrillators. External defibrillators deliver electric shocks to restore normal heart rhythm. The "Others" category includes additional medical equipment used for cardiac monitoring, diagnosis, and treatment.</p></p>
