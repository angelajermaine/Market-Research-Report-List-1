<p><p>Anti-static overalls are protective clothing designed to prevent static electricity buildup in various industries. The market offers two types of overalls: split type and siamese type. Split type overalls consist of separate upper and lower garment pieces that can be worn independently. Siamese type overalls, on the other hand, are a one-piece suit with no separation between the upper and lower parts. Both types provide effective protection against static electricity, but the choice depends on the user's preference and specific workplace requirements.</p></p>
