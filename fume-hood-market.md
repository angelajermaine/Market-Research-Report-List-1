<p><p>Fume Hoods are equipment used in laboratories to protect the user from harmful fumes, vapors, and chemicals. The market for Fume Hoods can be classified into two main types: Ductless Fume Hoods and Ducted Fume Hoods. </p><p>Ductless Fume Hoods have a built-in filtration system that cleans the air, allowing it to be released back into the laboratory. They are cost-effective and suitable for small spaces but have limited capacity and require regular maintenance. </p><p>Ducted Fume Hoods, on the other hand, are connected to an external exhaust system. They provide efficient removal of hazardous substances but are expensive and require extensive installation. They are commonly used in larger laboratories or facilities.</p></p>
