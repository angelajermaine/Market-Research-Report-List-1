<p><p>Pedal sensors are components used to detect and measure the movement of pedals in various applications. There are three main types of pedal sensors in the market. Resistive sensors use a resistive film that changes resistance when pressure is applied. Magnetic sensors use magnets and Hall Effect sensors to measure changes in magnetic field strength. Inductive sensors work on the principle of electromagnetic induction to detect pedal movement. These sensors are essential in industries like automotive, gaming, and fitness equipment, enabling accurate and reliable pedal control.</p></p>
