<p><p>An orbital hydraulic motor, also known as a low-speed high-torque (LSHT) motor, is a type of hydraulic component that converts fluid power into rotational mechanical power. It works by using fluid pressure to push a series of gears or gerotors around a central shaft, generating torque in the process. These motors are widely used in various industrial applications such as construction equipment, agriculture machinery, mining equipment, and marine vessels.</p><p>The market for orbital hydraulic motors has witnessed steady growth in recent years and is expected to continue growing at a compound annual growth rate (CAGR) of 7.6% during the forecast period.
