Albumin is a protein used to treat low blood volume. Prothrombin Complex Concentrates are used to reverse the effects of blood thinners. Antithrombin Concentrates are used for patients with low antithrombin levels. Factor XIII Concentrates are utilized for patients with Factor XIII deficiency. Lastly, Fibrinogen Concentrates are used to treat bleeding disorders by boosting fibrinogen levels in the blood. These therapies play vital roles in critical care scenarios.</p></p>
