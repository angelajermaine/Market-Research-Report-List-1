The increasing demand for safe and secure packaging solutions across different industries is the major driving factor for market growth. Furthermore, the rising awareness about environmental sustainability and the adoption of eco-friendly packaging materials have led to the development of biodegradable polyethylene liners, which are expected to bolster market growth.</p><p>The market is also witnessing several trends that are shaping its growth.
