<p><p>The Immunoassay market includes various types of technologies used for detecting and measuring specific substances in biological samples. These technologies can be categorized into different types such as ELISA (Enzyme-Linked Immunosorbent Assay), which uses enzymes to detect antigens or antibodies; Rapid Tests, which provide quick results using lateral flow or dipstick formats; Western Blotting, which detects specific proteins; ELISPOT, which measures cytokine secretion from individual cells; Immuno-PCR, which combines immunoassay and polymerase chain reaction; and Other Technologies, which include fluorescence immunoassays, chemiluminescence immunoassays, and radioimmunoassays.</p></p>
