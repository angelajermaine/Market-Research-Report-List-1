<p><p>The outdoor step sensor market includes two main types of sensors: gravity sensors and vibration sensors. Gravity sensors detect changes in the gravitational force as users move, allowing for accurate step counting. They are commonly used in fitness trackers and smartwatches. On the other hand, vibration sensors detect subtle vibrations created by foot strikes during walking or running. This type of sensor is often used in smart shoes or insoles to track steps and gait analysis. Both types of sensors contribute to the accurate measurement of outdoor activities.</p></p>
