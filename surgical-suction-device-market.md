<p><p>The Surgical Suction Device Market includes two main types: Electric Suction Devices and Pneumatic Suction Devices. Electric suction devices use an electric motor to create suction, making them suitable for continuous and high-volume suctioning needs. On the other hand, pneumatic suction devices are powered by compressed air or gas, making them more portable and flexible for various medical procedures. Both types of devices are widely used in surgical settings to remove fluids, gases, and other unwanted substances from the patient's body during surgeries or medical procedures.</p></p>
