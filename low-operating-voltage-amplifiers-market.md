<p><p>Low operating voltage amplifiers are available in three market types: single operator, dual operator, and quad operator. </p><p>Single operator amplifiers are designed to be operated by a single person and provide amplification for low voltage applications. They are suitable for smaller-scale projects or personal use.</p><p>Dual operator amplifiers, as the name suggests, can be operated by two people simultaneously. They offer enhanced functionality and are ideal for applications that require more control or multiple inputs.</p><p>Quad operator amplifiers can be operated by four individuals at the same time. These amplifiers provide even greater versatility and are commonly used in professional settings or complex systems that require multiple operators to work together efficiently.</p></p>
