<p><p>The self-driving forklift market can be categorized into three main types: vision navigation, lidar navigation, and others. Vision navigation relies on cameras and sensors to capture and interpret visual information, allowing the forklift to navigate autonomously. Lidar navigation uses laser sensors to measure distances and create a 3D map of the forklift's surroundings, enabling precise navigation. The "Others" category includes alternative navigation technologies like ultrasonic sensors or magnetic tape guidance systems. These different market types offer distinct approaches to self-driving forklifts, each with its own advantages and applications.</p></p>
