<p><strong>The Automotive Double-Clutch Transmission Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Wet Multiplate Clutches</li><li>Dry Single-Plate Clutches</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automotive double-clutch transmissions come in two main types: those with wet multiplate clutches and those with dry single-plate clutches. Wet multiplate clutch systems use oil for cooling and lubrication, making them more efficient and durable, but also more complex and costly. Dry single-plate clutch systems, on the other hand, do not use oil, making them lighter and simpler, but they can be more prone to wear and overheating. Both types have their advantages and are used in various vehicles based on specific requirements.</p></p>
