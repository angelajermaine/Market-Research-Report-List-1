<p><p>The Steelmaking Electric Arc Furnace market is comprised of three types: Furnace Body Open Type, Stove Top Open Type, and Furnace Top Rotary. The Furnace Body Open Type refers to an electric arc furnace with an open body design, allowing easy access for materials and charging. The Stove Top Open Type also has an open top design, allowing efficient charging and heat transfer. Lastly, the Furnace Top Rotary is a type of electric arc furnace with a rotating top, enabling optimal heat distribution and stirring of the materials during the steelmaking process.</p></p>
