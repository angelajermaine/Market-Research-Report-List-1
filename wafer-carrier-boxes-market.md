<p><ul><li>Front Opening Shipping Box (FOSB)</li><li>Front Opening Unified Pod (FOUP)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Wafer carrier boxes are used for transporting and storing silicon wafers in the semiconductor industry. Front Opening Shipping Box (FOSB) is a type of wafer carrier box with a front opening door for easy access to the wafers. Front Opening Unified Pod (FOUP) is another type of wafer carrier box that is used for transporting wafers in a cleanroom environment. Both FOSB and FOUP are essential in ensuring the safe and secure transportation of delicate silicon wafers in the semiconductor industry.</p></p>
