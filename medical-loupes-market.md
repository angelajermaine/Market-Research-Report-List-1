<p><strong>The Medical Loupes Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Through The Lens Loupe (TTL)</li><li>Flip Up Loupe</li><li>Galilean Loupe</li><li>Prismatic Loupe</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Medical loupes are used by healthcare professionals to magnify their view during procedures. Through The Lens Loupe (TTL) is a type that provides a direct and wider field of view, with custom adjustments for individual eyes. Flip Up Loupe allows users to easily switch between magnified and normal vision. Galilean Loupe is compact, lightweight, and offers a wider field of view, but with less magnification. Prismatic Loupe uses prism optics to provide a wider field of view and greater depth perception, but it can be heavier and more expensive. Each type has its benefits and drawbacks, catering to different professionals' needs.</p></p>
