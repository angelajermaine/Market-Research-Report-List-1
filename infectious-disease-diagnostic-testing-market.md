<p><p>The infectious disease diagnostic testing market consists of two main types: molecular diagnostic tests and point-of-care (POC) infectious disease diagnostics tests. </p><p>Molecular diagnostic tests involve the detection of specific DNA or RNA sequences associated with infectious agents. These tests are typically performed in laboratory settings and provide accurate and sensitive results.</p><p>On the other hand, POC infectious disease diagnostics tests are designed to be used at the patient's side, providing rapid and convenient results. These tests are often used in remote or resource-limited settings, enabling immediate diagnosis and treatment decisions.</p></p>
