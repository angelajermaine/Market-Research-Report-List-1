<p><p>The screen printer market is divided into two main types: fully automatic and semi-automatic. Fully automatic screen printers are highly efficient and require minimal human intervention. They can handle large volumes of printing jobs and are characterized by their speed, precision, and advanced features. On the other hand, semi-automatic screen printers require more human involvement in terms of loading and unloading the substrate. While they are not as fast as fully automatic printers, they are more affordable and suitable for small to medium-scale printing operations.</p></p>
