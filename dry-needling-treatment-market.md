<p><p>Dry needling treatment is a technique used by physical therapists, chiropractors, and other healthcare professionals to relieve pain and increase mobility by inserting thin needles into musculoskeletal trigger points. These trigger points are tight knots of muscle fibers that can lead to pain and dysfunction. Dry needling aims to disrupt these trigger points, releasing tension and promoting healing.</p><p>The global dry needling treatment market is experiencing significant growth, driven by factors like the rising prevalence of musculoskeletal disorders, growing adoption of minimally invasive procedures, and increasing demand for alternative therapies. This technology-driven approach is gaining traction, as it allows for better targeting of trigger points and improved outcomes.</p><p>Furthermore, there is an increasing focus on the evidence-based practice of dry needling.
