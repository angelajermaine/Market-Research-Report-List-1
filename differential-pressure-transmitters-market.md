<p><p>The differential pressure transmitters market can be broadly categorized into two types: digital type and analog type. Digital type transmitters use digital technology and provide accurate and precise measurements of pressure differentials. They offer advanced features like built-in communication protocols and data logging capabilities. On the other hand, analog type transmitters use analog technology, providing measurement outputs in the form of electrical signals proportional to the pressure difference. Although analog transmitters are less precise compared to digital ones, they are more commonly used due to their simplicity, affordability, and compatibility with existing systems.</p></p>
