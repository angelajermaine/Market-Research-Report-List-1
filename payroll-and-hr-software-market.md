<p><p>The payroll and HR software market can be categorized into two types: on-premise and cloud-based solutions. On-premise software refers to a traditional model where the software is installed locally on the company's servers and managed by the internal IT department. In contrast, cloud-based software is accessed through the internet and hosted on remote servers, requiring no local installation or maintenance. Cloud-based solutions offer the advantages of scalability, accessibility from anywhere, and cost-effectiveness, while on-premise solutions provide greater control and customization options but require higher upfront investment and maintenance.</p></p>
