<p><p>Walk-in bathtubs are categorized into two main types: embedded bathtubs and independent bathtubs. </p><p>Embedded bathtubs are designed to be installed within a bathroom's existing layout. They typically require modifications to the bathroom floor or wall for installation. These bathtubs are seamlessly integrated into the bathroom design, offering a more aesthetically pleasing look.</p><p>On the other hand, independent bathtubs are self-contained units that do not require any modifications to the bathroom. They are freestanding and can be easily moved or relocated within the bathroom. These bathtubs offer more flexibility in terms of placement and are suitable for bathrooms where structural modifications are not feasible.</p></p>
