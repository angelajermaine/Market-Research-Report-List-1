<p><p>Rigid sterilization containers are used to safely sterilize and store medical instruments. Filter type containers are equipped with air filters that prevent the entry of contaminants during sterilization. These containers maintain the sterility of instruments even after opening. On the other hand, valve type containers feature a pressure relief valve that allows for steam sterilization, without the need for a separate filter, ensuring efficient sterilization and convenience. Both types of containers are widely utilized in medical settings to ensure the safety and sterility of instruments.</p></p>
