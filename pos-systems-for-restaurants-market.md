<p><p>POS Systems for Restaurants come in two main market types: cloud-based and on-premises.</p><p>Cloud-based systems are software solutions that are hosted on remote servers and accessed through the internet. They offer easy accessibility, scalability, and real-time data synchronization, making them suitable for restaurants of all sizes.</p><p>On-premises systems, on the other hand, are installed locally within the restaurant's premises. They require physical hardware and infrastructure to operate and may involve higher upfront costs, but provide more control over data security and customization options.</p><p>Both types have their advantages and it ultimately depends on the specific needs and preferences of the restaurant.</p></p>
