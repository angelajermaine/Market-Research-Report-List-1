<p><p>The PVC-sheathed power cables market consists of three main types: cooper core, aluminum core, and others. Cooper core refers to power cables in which the conductive core is made of copper, providing excellent conductivity and durability. Aluminum core power cables have a conductive core made of aluminum, offering a more cost-effective option. The "others" category includes power cables with cores made from materials other than copper or aluminum, such as steel or alloy. These different types cater to various needs and budgets within the PVC-sheathed power cables market.</p></p>
