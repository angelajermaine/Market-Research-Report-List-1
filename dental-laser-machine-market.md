<p><p>The dental laser machine market can be categorized into two types: soft tissue dental lasers and all tissue dental lasers. On the other hand, all tissue dental lasers can be used for both soft and hard tissues, making them suitable for a wider range of treatments including cavity preparation, bone re-contouring, and even teeth whitening. These different types of dental lasers provide dentists with versatile tools to perform various dental procedures with precision and minimal discomfort for patients.</p></p>
