<p><p>Radiation pyrometers are temperature measurement devices that rely on the principle of thermal radiation to determine the temperature of an object. There are two main types of radiation pyrometers: reflective type and refraction type. </p><p>Reflective type pyrometers use a mirror to redirect the thermal radiation emitted by the object towards a detector, allowing temperature measurement. Refraction type pyrometers, on the other hand, use a lens or an optical system to focus the thermal radiation onto a detector. Both types can accurately measure high temperatures in various industrial applications, offering reliable temperature monitoring solutions.</p></p>
