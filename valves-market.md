<p><ul><li>Gate Valves</li><li>Control Valves</li><li>Ball Valves</li><li>Butterfly Valve</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Valves are commonly used to control the flow of liquids or gases in various industries. Gate valves regulate the flow with a sliding gate mechanism, while control valves accurately manage the flow rate and pressure. Ball valves use a spherical disc for efficient flow control, while butterfly valves have a rotating disc for on/off control. There are also other types of valves available, including check valves, diaphragm valves, and globe valves, each designed for specific applications in industries like oil and gas, water treatment, and manufacturing.</p></p>
