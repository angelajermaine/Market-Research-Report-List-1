<p><p>Social customer service software is available in two market types, namely on-premises and cloud-based. On-premises software refers to a system that is installed and operated on the premises of the company using it. This requires managing the infrastructure, maintenance, and upgrades internally. In contrast, cloud-based software is hosted remotely on the service provider's servers and accessed through the internet. Users do not need to manage the infrastructure as updates and maintenance are handled by the provider. Cloud-based solutions offer more flexibility, scalability, and cost-effectiveness compared to on-premises options.</p></p>
