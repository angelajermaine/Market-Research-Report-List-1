<p><p>Solid electrolyte batteries are a type of battery that uses a solid material as its electrolyte instead of the liquid or gel electrolyte used in traditional batteries. Two main types of solid electrolyte batteries are polymer-based solid state batteries and solid state batteries with inorganic solid electrolytes. Polymer-based solid state batteries use a polymer as the solid electrolyte, while solid state batteries with inorganic solid electrolytes use inorganic materials such as ceramics as the solid electrolyte. Both types offer advantages such as improved safety, higher energy density, and longer lifespan compared to traditional batteries.</p></p>
