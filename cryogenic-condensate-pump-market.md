<p><p>The Cryogenic Condensate Pump market consists of three types of pumps: Positive Displacement Pump, Power Pump, and Retention Pump. </p><p>Positive Displacement Pump is a type of pump that moves a fixed volume of fluid from the inlet to the outlet by trapping and releasing it through a rotating mechanism. </p><p>Power Pump is a pump that utilizes centrifugal force to move the fluid by converting mechanical energy into kinetic energy. </p><p>Retention Pump is a pump that maintains a constant flow of fluid by continuously removing any excess fluid and returning it to the source or storage.</p></p>
