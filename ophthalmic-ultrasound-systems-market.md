<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Ophthalmic Ultrasound Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>A-Scan</li><li>B-Scan</li><li>Combined Scan</li><li>Pachymetry</li><li>Ultrasound Bio Microscope (UBM)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Ophthalmic Ultrasound Systems Market includes various types of ultrasound scans used in eye examinations. A-Scan measures the length of the eye, helping in diagnosing conditions like cataract or intraocular lens calculations. B-Scan uses sound waves to create cross-sectional images of the eye, detecting retinal detachments. Combined Scan integrates A-Scan and B-Scan capabilities. Pachymetry measures corneal thickness to diagnose conditions like glaucoma. Ultrasound Bio Microscope (UBM) provides high-resolution images of anterior eye structures, aiding in diagnosing and treating conditions like tumors or iridocorneal adhesions. These systems help facilitate accurate diagnoses and treatment plans for eye-related conditions.</p></p>
