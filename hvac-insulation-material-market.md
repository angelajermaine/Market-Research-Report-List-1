<p><p>The HVAC insulation material market consists of various types, including glass wool, stone wool, elastomeric foam, XPS (extruded polystyrene), and others. Glass wool is made from recycled glass and is commonly used for heat insulation. Stone wool, made from volcanic rock, offers excellent fire resistance and noise reduction properties. Elastomeric foam is a flexible material that provides good thermal insulation and condensation control. XPS is a rigid foam insulation board that provides high compressive strength and moisture resistance. The "Others" category encompasses various insulation materials not specifically mentioned.</p></p>
