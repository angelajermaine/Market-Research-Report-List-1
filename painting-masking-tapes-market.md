<p><p>Painting masking tapes are classified into four main types based on their adhesive composition. Silicon-based tapes offer high temperature resistance and are suitable for professional painting applications. Acrylic-based tapes are versatile and provide good adhesion on various surfaces. Rubber-based tapes are known for their flexibility and easy removal without leaving residue. Vinyl tapes are long-lasting and offer strong adhesion, making them ideal for outdoor use. Each type caters to different needs and preferences in the painting industry.</p></p>
