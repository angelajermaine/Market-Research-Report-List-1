<p><p>A tray food dehydrator is a kitchen appliance used for removing moisture from food items like fruits, vegetables, and meats, to extend their shelf life and preserve their nutritional content. Consumers are becoming more health-conscious and are opting for dehydrated foods as they are free from preservatives and additives, making them a convenient and nutritious snacking option.</p><p>Moreover, the rising popularity of DIY food processing and growing concerns about food waste reduction are also driving the market growth.
