<p><strong>The Contact Grills Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Liquid Propane (LP) Grills</li><li>Natural Gas (NG) Grills</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Contact grills come in two variations: liquid propane (LP) grills and natural gas (NG) grills. LP grills operate using propane gas, which is stored in tanks and easily refillable. They offer versatile usage, making them suitable for both indoor and outdoor cooking. NG grills, on the other hand, are connected directly to a natural gas line, making them a convenient and reliable option for homes or commercial establishments with a gas supply. Both LP and NG grills provide efficient and reliable cooking options for users, with LP grills offering greater mobility and NG grills providing a continuous gas supply.</p></p>
