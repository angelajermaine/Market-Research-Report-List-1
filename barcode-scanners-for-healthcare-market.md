<p><p>Barcode scanners for healthcare come in two main types: wired and wireless. Wired barcode scanners are connected to a computer or device through a physical cable, providing a reliable and secure connection. They are ideal for fixed workstations or areas where mobility is not required. On the other hand, wireless barcode scanners use Bluetooth or Wi-Fi technology to transmit data wirelessly to a computer or device. This allows healthcare professionals to move freely around the facility while still being able to scan and track information efficiently.</p></p>
