<p><p>Fans for smoke extraction are used to remove smoke and other harmful gases from buildings during fire incidents. There are two main types of smoke fans: axial smoke fans and centrifugal smoke fans. Axial smoke fans consist of rotating blades that draw air directly along the axis of rotation, creating high airflow rates. On the other hand, centrifugal smoke fans use rotating impellers to push air outwards from the center, generating high pressures. Both types are essential in ensuring efficient smoke extraction and maintaining safe environments during emergencies.</p></p>
