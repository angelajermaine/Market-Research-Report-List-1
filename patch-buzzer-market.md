<p><p>Patch buzzer is a type of audio device used for generating sound signals. There are two main market types for patch buzzers: active and passive. Active patch buzzers require an external power source to operate and produce sound. They offer higher output volume and are commonly used in applications where a louder sound is required. On the other hand, passive patch buzzers do not require any external power and generate sound through the vibration of their diaphragms. They are often used in low-power applications where a softer sound is sufficient.</p></p>
