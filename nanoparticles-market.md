<p><p>Nanoparticles are categorised into different types based on their composition and applications. Liposomes are nanoparticles made up of lipid bilayers, used for drug delivery. Polymer-conjugated drugs are nanoparticles where drugs are attached to polymers, improving their solubility and uptake. Polymeric nanoparticles are made of biodegradable polymers for drug delivery. Dendrimers are highly branched molecules used for drug delivery and imaging. Inorganic nanoparticles are made up of metals or metal oxides, utilized in various fields such as electronics and medicine. Additionally, there are several other types of nanoparticles with diverse compositions and applications in different industries.</p></p>
