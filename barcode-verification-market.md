Barcode verification helps companies improve operational efficiency, reduce errors, and enhance supply chain visibility, thereby promoting its adoption.</p><p>In addition, stringent government regulations and standards mandating the use of high-quality barcodes in certain industries are further fueling the demand for barcode verification solutions. <p><p>Barcode Verification market can be categorized into different types based on the barcode used and the product used for verification. The types based on barcode include One-Dimensional (1D) Barcode Verification, which assesses 1D barcodes like UPC or EAN codes, and Two-Dimensional (2D) Barcode Verification, which evaluates barcodes with more complex data storage like QR codes. Another type is Direct Part Marking (DPM) Barcode Verification, which verifies barcodes directly marked on products. Based on the product, there are Portable Barcode Verifiers, compact devices for on-the-go verification, and Desktop Barcode Verifiers, larger systems typically used in stationary settings.</p></p>
