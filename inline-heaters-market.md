<p><p>Inline heaters are used in various industrial processes to heat fluids that pass through a pipeline. There are different types of inline heaters available in the market. Flat inline heaters are compact and provide uniform heat distribution. Molded-to-shape inline heaters are custom-designed to fit specific pipe configurations. Spiral-wrap inline heaters have a spiral heating element that wraps around the pipe to maximize heat transfer. Other types of inline heaters may include immersion-style, circulation, or tubular heaters, each with their own unique characteristics and applications.</p></p>
