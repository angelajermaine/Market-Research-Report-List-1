<p><p>Tool post grinders are used to achieve precision grinding on machine tools. There are two main types of tool post grinders: internal and external. Internal tool post grinders are designed for grinding internal surfaces of cylindrical components, such as bores or holes. They are commonly used in industries like automotive and aerospace. On the other hand, external tool post grinders are used for grinding external surfaces, such as shafts or outer diameters of cylindrical components. Both types of grinders help improve machining accuracy and achieve desired surface finishes.</p></p>
