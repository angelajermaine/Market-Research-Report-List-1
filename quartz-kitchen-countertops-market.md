<p><strong>The Quartz Kitchen Countertops Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Press Molding</li><li>Casting Molding</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Quartz kitchen countertops are available in two main market types: press molding and casting molding. Press molding involves compressing a mixture of quartz crystals, resins, and pigments under high pressure to create solid slabs. This method offers a wide range of colors and designs, making it a popular choice among homeowners. On the other hand, casting molding involves pouring the quartz mixture into molds and allowing it to cure. This method allows for more intricate designs and customization but may be more time-consuming and expensive.</p></p>
