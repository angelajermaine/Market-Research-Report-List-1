<p><p>The Diesel Oxidation Catalytic Converter (DOC) market consists of two primary types: Metallic DOC Technology and Ceramic DOC Technology. Metallic DOC Technology utilizes a metal substrate coated with catalyst material to convert harmful pollutants into less harmful substances. This type of converter is known for its durability and lower cost. On the other hand, Ceramic DOC Technology employs a ceramic substrate coated with catalyst material, making it more efficient in pollutant conversion and offering a longer lifespan, but at a higher price point.</p></p>
