<p><strong>The Haptic Feedback Surgical System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Kinesthetic Feedback System</li><li>Cutaneous Feedback System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Haptic Feedback Surgical System market can be categorized into two types: Kinesthetic Feedback System and Cutaneous Feedback System. </p><p>A Kinesthetic Feedback System provides surgeons with force and position feedback, allowing them to sense the movement and resistance of surgical instruments. This helps improve precision and accuracy during surgical procedures. </p><p>In contrast, a Cutaneous Feedback System provides tactile sensations to the surgeon, such as pressure or vibration, enhancing their ability to perceive tissue characteristics and make informed decisions during surgery. Both these systems contribute to enhancing the overall surgical experience and outcomes.</p></p>
