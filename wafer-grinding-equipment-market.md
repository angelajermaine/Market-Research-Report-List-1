<p><p>The wafer grinding equipment market consists of two types: wafer edge grinder and wafer surface grinder. A wafer edge grinder is used to grind the edge of the wafer to improve its strength and prevent chipping. On the other hand, a wafer surface grinder is used to flatten and smooth the surface of the wafer, ensuring a high level of precision and eliminating any defects. These equipment are essential in the semiconductor manufacturing industry to produce high-quality wafers for various electronic applications.</p></p>
