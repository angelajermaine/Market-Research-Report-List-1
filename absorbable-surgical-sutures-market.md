They provide support to the wound during the healing process and eventually lose their strength and dissolve.</p><p>The absorbable surgical sutures market is expected to witness significant growth during the forecast period. <p><p>Absorbable surgical sutures refer to the types of sutures used during surgical procedures that can be naturally broken down and absorbed by the body over time. There are two main types of absorbable sutures: monofilament sutures and multifilament sutures. Monofilament sutures are made of a single strand of material, ensuring minimal tissue reaction and less risk of infection. On the other hand, multifilament sutures consist of multiple intertwined strands, providing better handling and knot security. Both types have their advantages and are used based on the specific requirements of the surgical procedure.</p></p>
