<p><p>The high-performance MEMS based inertial sensors market consists of various types of sensors. Accelerometers measure acceleration forces, commonly used in automotive and aerospace applications for stability control. Gyroscopes measure rotational motion and are utilized in image stabilization and navigation systems. Inertial combo sensors combine both accelerometer and gyroscope functionalities, allowing for comprehensive motion detection in electronic devices. Magnetometers are used to measure magnetic fields and find applications in compasses and navigation systems. These sensors play a crucial role in enabling accurate motion tracking and positioning in a wide range of industries.</p></p>
