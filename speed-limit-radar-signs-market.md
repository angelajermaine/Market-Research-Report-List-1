<p><p>The speed limit radar signs market can be classified into three types based on their power sources. AC power indicates signs that are connected to an electrical power source and are generally used in permanent installations. Solar power radar signs have integrated solar panels that convert sunlight into electricity, allowing them to operate independently of grid power, making them ideal for remote areas or locations without access to electricity.</p></p>
