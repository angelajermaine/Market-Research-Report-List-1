<p><p>PSA (Pressure Swing Adsorption) hydrogen purification is a technology used to separate hydrogen gas from impurities and purify it for various industrial applications. This process involves the use of adsorbent materials that selectively adsorb impurities while allowing hydrogen to pass through. One of the key factors driving the market growth is the increasing demand for hydrogen in various sectors, including fuel cells, refining, and chemical manufacturing.
