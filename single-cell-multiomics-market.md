Single cell multiomics has emerged as a powerful tool in various fields, including cancer research, neuroscience, immunology, and developmental biology.</p><p>The single cell multiomics market has witnessed significant growth in recent years and is expected to continue to grow at a CAGR of 11% during the forecast period.
