<p><p>The UV PCR workstation market can be categorized into three types: Multiplex PCR, Single-cell PCR, and Others. Multiplex PCR refers to the simultaneous amplification of multiple DNA targets within a single PCR reaction, enabling efficient and cost-effective analysis of multiple genes. Single-cell PCR allows for the amplification of DNA from individual cells, allowing researchers to study genetic variations at a single-cell level. The "Others" category comprises other PCR techniques, such as real-time PCR or digital PCR, which have unique applications in various research fields.</p></p>
