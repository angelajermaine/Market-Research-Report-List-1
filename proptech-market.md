<p><ul><li>On-premise</li><li>Cloud-based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>PropTech market can generally be categorized into two types: on-premise and cloud-based. On-premise solutions refer to software or systems that are installed and operated within a company's own physical infrastructure. This requires companies to maintain their own servers and infrastructure. Conversely, cloud-based solutions are delivered over the internet and hosted on remote servers, allowing companies to access and operate software applications and data remotely. Cloud-based solutions offer flexibility, scalability, and often cost savings due to the elimination of upfront infrastructure costs.</p></p>
