<p><strong>The Particle Counter for Liquids Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Offline Type</li><li>In-line Remote Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Particle counters for liquids are used to measure and analyze the number and size distribution of particles suspended in a liquid. There are two main types of these devices: offline type and in-line remote type. The offline type is designed to be used outside the liquid pipeline, allowing for ease of sampling and analysis separately from the liquid flow. On the other hand, the in-line remote type is installed directly in the liquid pipeline, providing real-time monitoring and measurement of particles without the need for manual sampling.</p></p>
