Electrosurgery generators are the power units that produce the electrical current for surgical procedures. Electrosurgery instruments are the handheld devices used by surgeons to perform the actual tissue manipulation. Accessories include various tools and components such as electrodes, cables, foot switches, and adapters that are necessary for the proper functioning of the electrosurgery system.</p></p>
