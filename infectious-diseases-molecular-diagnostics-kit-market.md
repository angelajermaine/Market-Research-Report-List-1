<p><p>The Infectious Diseases Molecular Diagnostics Kit market includes various types of testing methods. Polymerase Chain Reaction (PCR) is a widely used method that amplifies DNA sequences, enabling the detection of specific pathogens. Molecular Hybridization involves the complementary pairing of DNA or RNA strands to identify targeted genetic material. Biochips are devices that analyze multiple DNA samples simultaneously and provide results. Other techniques may include next-generation sequencing, microarrays, and isothermal nucleic acid amplification. These diverse methods collectively aid in the detection and diagnosis of infectious diseases, helping healthcare professionals make informed treatment decisions.</p></p>
