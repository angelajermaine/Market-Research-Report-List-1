<p><p>Medical processing seals are essential components in medical devices and equipment, ensuring their proper functioning and preventing any leakage or contamination. There are various types of seals used in the medical processing industry. O-rings are circular seals made of elastomer that create a tight seal between two parts. Gaskets are flat seals typically made of rubber or silicone, used to prevent fluid or gas leakage. Lip seals, also known as oil seals, are used to prevent lubricant leakage in rotating machinery. These seals play a vital role in maintaining the integrity of medical processing equipment.</p></p>
