<p><strong>The Medical Grade Hydroxypropyl Cellulose Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>L-HPC</li><li>H-HPC</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Medical Grade Hydroxypropyl Cellulose (HPC) is classified into two types: L-HPC and H-HPC. L-HPC is low-substituted, while H-HPC is high-substituted. L-HPC is commonly used as a binder and film former in pharmaceutical formulations, whereas H-HPC is primarily utilized for sustained-release drug delivery systems. Both types of HPC play a crucial role in the pharmaceutical industry due to their unique properties such as film-forming ability, solubility in water, and biocompatibility, making them ideal materials for various medical applications.</p></p>
