<p><p>The light duty conveyor belt market is segmented into four types: thermoplastic covered, solid plastics, lightweight rubber, and non-woven belting. Thermoplastic covered belts are made from polyethylene and provide excellent resistance to chemicals and abrasions. Solid plastics belts are composed of interlocking plastic modules and are suitable for transporting small items. Lightweight rubber belts are made from synthetic rubber materials and are commonly used in industries like food processing. Non-woven belting is made from polyester and is suitable for light-duty applications that require flexibility and low noise levels.</p></p>
