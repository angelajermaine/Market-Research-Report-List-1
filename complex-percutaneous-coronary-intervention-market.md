<p><p>Complex Percutaneous Coronary Intervention (CPCI) refers to a specialized procedure to treat heart disease. The market types in CPCI include Drug-Eluting Stents (DES), which release medication to prevent the reoccurrence of blockages; Bare Metal Stents, which provide a mesh-like support structure to keep arteries open; and Bioresorbable Stents, which gradually dissolve over time. DES are popular due to their efficacy, while Bare Metal Stents are suitable for patients with shorter treatment duration. Bioresorbable Stents offer advantages of complete dissipation and avoiding long-term complications associated with permanent implants.</p></p>
