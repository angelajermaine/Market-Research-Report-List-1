<p><p>Construction bid management software can be classified into two types: on-premise and cloud-based. On-premise bid management software is installed and run on the user's local servers, offering more control and customization options, but requiring maintenance and infrastructure costs. On the other hand, cloud-based bid management software operates via the internet, allowing users to access it from any location with an internet connection. It offers scalability, automatic updates, and lower upfront costs, as the software provider handles maintenance and infrastructure.</p></p>
