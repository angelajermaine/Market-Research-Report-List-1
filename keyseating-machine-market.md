<p><p>Keyseating machines can be classified into two main market types: automatic and semi-automatic machines. </p><p>Automatic keyseating machines are designed to perform all keyseating operations automatically, without requiring much intervention from the operator. These machines are equipped with advanced features and programming capabilities that allow them to execute precise and complex keyseating tasks efficiently.</p><p>On the other hand, semi-automatic keyseating machines require some level of manual input from the operator. While these machines may have certain automated functions, operators still play a significant role in setting up the workpiece and initiating the keyseating process. Semi-automatic machines are generally more affordable and suitable for smaller-scale applications.</p></p>
