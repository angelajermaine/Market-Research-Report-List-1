<p><strong>The Laminated Wheels Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Bonded Type</li><li>Non-bonded Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Laminated Wheels refer to wheels used in various industrial applications. They are divided into two main market types: Bonded Type and Non-bonded Type. </p><p>In the Bonded Type market, the wheels are manufactured by bonding multiple layers of materials together, such as rubber and fabric. This bonding process strengthens the wheel's structure and enhances its durability, making it suitable for heavy-duty operations that require reliable performance.</p><p>On the other hand, the Non-bonded Type market involves wheels that are constructed without any bonding process. Instead, the layers of materials are held together through alternative methods, such as mechanical fastening or interlocking designs. This type of wheel offers flexibility in terms of customization and replacement of individual layers, making it more versatile for diverse applications.</p></p>
