<p><strong>The Surgical Instrument Tracking Software Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>RFID</li><li>Barcode</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Surgical Instrument Tracking Software Market comprises two main types, namely RFID and Barcode systems. RFID (Radio Frequency Identification) uses small tags attached to the surgical instruments that communicate wirelessly with readers, allowing for real-time tracking and monitoring. On the other hand, Barcode systems utilize unique codes attached to each instrument, with the information scanned by a barcode reader. Both types of tracking systems provide hospitals and healthcare facilities with efficient inventory management, accurate instrument identification, and overall improved patient safety during surgical procedures.</p></p>
