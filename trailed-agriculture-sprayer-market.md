<p><p>Trailed agriculture sprayers are commonly classified into two types: engine drive sprayers and motor drive sprayers. Engine drive sprayers are powered by combustion engines, such as gas or diesel, to operate the pump and spray nozzles. Motor drive sprayers, on the other hand, are powered by electric motors. Both types of sprayers are used in agricultural settings to apply pesticides, herbicides, and fertilizers to crops. Engine drive sprayers are typically more powerful and suited for larger operations, while motor drive sprayers are more lightweight and portable.</p></p>
