<p><ul><li>Non-Cut-Off Luminaire</li><li>Semi-Cut-Off Luminaire</li><li>Full-Cut-Off Luminaire</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The road lighting fixtures market can be categorized into three types: non-cut-off luminaire, semi-cut-off luminaire, and full-cut-off luminaire. Non-cut-off luminaires emit light in all directions, making them suitable for general lighting purposes. Semi-cut-off luminaires direct most of the light downward, making them ideal for streets and highways. Full-cut-off luminaires have a precise light control system that ensures all light is directed downwards, minimizing light pollution and improving visibility. Each type is designed to meet specific lighting requirements and minimize the negative impact on the environment.</p></p>
