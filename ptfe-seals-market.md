<p><p>The PTFE seals market comprises various types of seals used in different applications. Rod seals are used to prevent leakage between the piston rod and cylinder head. O-rings are circular seals used to create a tight seal between two parts. Backup rings are designed to support and improve the effectiveness of O-rings. Other PTFE seals include wipers, guide rings, and rotary seals, each serving unique purposes such as dust and debris removal or enhanced rotary movement sealing. These seals are essential for ensuring leak-free operation in industries like automotive, manufacturing, and aerospace.</p></p>
