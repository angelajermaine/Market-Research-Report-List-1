<p><p>Sustainable toys, also known as eco-friendly toys, are playthings made from environmentally friendly materials, produced using sustainable manufacturing processes, and designed to minimize their carbon footprint. These materials are considered safer for children, as they do not contain harmful chemicals or toxins often found in conventional plastic toys. Wooden toys, in particular, are gaining popularity due to their durability, natural aesthetic, and ability to stimulate creativity and imagination.</p><p>Another trend in the sustainable toys market is the incorporation of elements of recycling and upcycling. Additionally, some manufacturers are designing toys that can be easily disassembled and recycled at the end of their lifespan, reducing waste and promoting a circular economy.</p><p>Overall, the sustainable toys market is witnessing significant growth, driven by increasing consumer awareness and demand for eco-friendly products.
