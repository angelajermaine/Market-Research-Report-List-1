<p><ul><li>Injection Acne Drugs</li><li>External Acne Drugs</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Acne drugs can be categorized into two main types: injection acne drugs and external acne drugs. Injection acne drugs are administered through injections, usually directly into the affected areas of the skin. They are designed to target the root causes of acne and reduce inflammation. On the other hand, external acne drugs are applied topically onto the skin. They come in the form of creams, gels, lotions, or ointments, and work by killing bacteria, reducing oil production, and unclogging pores. Both types aim to treat acne and improve the overall appearance of the skin.</p></p>
