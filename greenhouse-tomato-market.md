Firstly, the rising demand for fresh, high-quality tomatoes throughout the year has driven the market growth. Additionally, the increasing awareness about the health benefits associated with tomatoes, such as a rich source of vitamins, antioxidants, and lycopene, has further fueled market growth.</p><p>Moreover, the adoption of advanced technologies and innovations in greenhouse farming techniques has significantly contributed to the market's expansion.
