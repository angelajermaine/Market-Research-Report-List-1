<p><p>Disposable vinyl gloves are available in two main types: powered vinyl gloves and powder-free vinyl gloves. Powered vinyl gloves are coated with a layer of powder, typically cornstarch, which helps in easier donning and removing of gloves. These gloves are commonly used in industries such as food processing and healthcare. On the other hand, powder-free vinyl gloves do not contain any added powder and are preferred in certain environments where the presence of powder may pose risks, such as clean rooms and laboratories. Both types of gloves are widely used for protection and hygiene purposes.</p></p>
