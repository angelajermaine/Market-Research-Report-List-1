<p><p>The Fire and Rescue Helmets Market can be divided into two main types: Structural Helmets and Jet-style Helmets. Structural Helmets are designed for firefighters who work in structural environments and are equipped with a durable outer shell, high-temperature resistance, and impact protection. Jet-style Helmets, on the other hand, are primarily used by rescue teams during aircraft incidents and are characterized by their lightweight design, superior ventilation, and clear visors for increased visibility. Both types serve the purpose of ensuring the safety and protection of firefighters and rescue personnel in different emergency scenarios.</p></p>
