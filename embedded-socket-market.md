<p><p>The embedded socket market includes various types such as pop-up socket, slide socket, panel socket, and others. A pop-up socket is designed to be hidden within a surface and can be revealed by simply pushing it down. A slide socket operates similarly, but instead of popping up, it slides out horizontally. Panel sockets are integrated into the surface, providing easy access to power sources. Other variants may include collapsible or rotating sockets. These different types cater to diverse user needs and preferences, offering convenient and space-saving solutions for power supply in various environments.</p></p>
