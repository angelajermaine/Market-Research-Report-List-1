<p><p>The AOP (Advanced Oxidation Process) system market consists of various types such as photochemical, catalytic wet, sonochemical, ozone, electrochemical, and Fenton systems. Photochemical AOP systems rely on light to generate reactive species, while catalytic wet systems utilize catalysts in a liquid phase. Sonochemical AOP systems employ ultrasound waves to generate reactive species, ozone systems use ozone gas, and electrochemical systems use electrical energy. Fenton AOP systems involve the generation of hydroxyl radicals through the reaction of iron and hydrogen peroxide. The complex market refers to the combination of multiple AOP systems for enhanced efficiency and effectiveness.</p></p>
