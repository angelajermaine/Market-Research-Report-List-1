<p><p>Orthopaedic suction sets are medical devices used in surgical procedures to remove liquids, debris, and gases from the surgical field. They come in two market types: straight and bent. A straight suction set is a straight tube connected to a suction container, allowing for easy insertion and removal of fluids. On the other hand, a bent suction set has a curved end that facilitates suction in difficult-to-reach areas or when the surgeon's access is limited. Both straight and bent suction sets perform the same function of maintaining a clear surgical field, but their designs cater to different surgical needs.</p></p>
