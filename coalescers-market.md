<p><strong>The Coalescers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Mechanical Coalescers</li><li>Electrostatic Coalescers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Coalescers are devices used in various industries to remove liquid droplets or solid particles from gas or liquid streams. Mechanical coalescers operate by employing filters or porous media to trap particles, allowing the clean fluid to pass through. Electrostatic coalescers, on the other hand, use an electric field to attract and capture the dispersed droplets or particles. Both mechanical and electrostatic coalescers are widely used in the market to enhance process efficiency and ensure product purity.</p></p>
