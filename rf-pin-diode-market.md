The market for RF PIN diodes can be categorized into two types: vertical PIN diodes and horizontal PIN diodes. Vertical PIN diodes are preferred for their high power handling capability and low capacitance, making them suitable for high-frequency applications. On the other hand, horizontal PIN diodes are known for their low resistance and low distortion, making them ideal for applications that require high linearity and low noise.</p></p>
