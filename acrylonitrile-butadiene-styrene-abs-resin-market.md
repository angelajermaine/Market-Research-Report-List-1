<p><p>The Acrylonitrile Butadiene Styrene (ABS) Resin market consists of two major types: Emulsion Graft Copolymerization and Bulk Copolymerization. Emulsion Graft Copolymerization involves the process of adding a graft monomer to the ABS resin emulsion, resulting in improved properties such as impact resistance and heat stability. On the other hand, Bulk Copolymerization involves combining ABS resin with other monomers in a bulk polymerization process, leading to the formation of ABS resin with different characteristics and properties.</p></p>
