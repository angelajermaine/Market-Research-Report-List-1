<p><p>Double feed weighers are used for weighing bulk products in the food industry. There are two main types of double feed weighers: gross weight scale and net weight scale. Gross weight scale measures the weight of both the product and the packaging material, while net weight scale measures only the weight of the product without the packaging. Both types are commonly employed in the market, with gross weight scale being more suitable for products that are sold with packaging and net weight scale being more appropriate for products sold without packaging.</p></p>
