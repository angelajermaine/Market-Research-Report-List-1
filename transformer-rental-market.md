<p><p>The transformer rental market is divided into two main types: low voltage transformers and high voltage transformers. Low voltage transformers are typically used in residential and commercial applications to step down the electrical voltage from the power grid to a lower level suitable for use in buildings. High voltage transformers, on the other hand, are employed in industrial settings or power generation facilities to step up the electrical voltage for transmission over long distances. Both types of transformers are rented by businesses or individuals who need temporary power supply solutions.</p></p>
