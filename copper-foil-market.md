<p><strong>The Copper Foil Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Rolled Copper Foil</li><li>Electrolytic Copper Foil</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Copper foil is available in two main market types: rolled copper foil and electrolytic copper foil. Rolled copper foil is produced by passing a copper ingot through a rolling mill, which results in a thinner and smoother foil used in industries like construction and electronics. On the other hand, electrolytic copper foil is made by depositing a layer of copper on a stainless steel cathode through an electrochemical process, resulting in a high-quality foil used in applications such as printed circuit boards and lithium-ion batteries.</p></p>
