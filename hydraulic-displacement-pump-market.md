<p><ul><li>Axial Piston Pumps</li><li>Radial Piston Pumps</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Hydraulic displacement pump market consists of two main types: Axial Piston Pumps and Radial Piston Pumps. Axial Piston Pumps are widely used due to their ability to handle high pressures and provide efficient performance. They feature pistons that move in an axial direction, creating pressure by displacing hydraulic fluid. On the other hand, Radial Piston Pumps have pistons arranged radially around a central shaft, which moves in a circular motion to displace fluid. Radial Piston Pumps are known for their high precision and accuracy in delivering fluid.</p></p>
