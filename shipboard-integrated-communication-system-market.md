<p><p>The Shipboard Integrated Communication System market includes two main types: On Board and On Shore. On Board refers to the communication systems installed on the ship, allowing smooth communication between crew members and the ship's operations. This includes various systems like internal telephone networks, intercoms, and public address systems. On Shore, on the other hand, refers to the communication systems that facilitate communication between the ship and the coast. This includes radio systems, satellite communication, and other technologies that enable ship-to-shore communication.</p></p>
