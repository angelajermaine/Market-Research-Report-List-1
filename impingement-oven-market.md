There are two main types of impingement ovens: conveyor impingement ovens and countertop impingement ovens. Conveyor impingement ovens are larger and designed for high-volume cooking in commercial settings, with a conveyor belt to move the food through the cooking chamber. Countertop impingement ovens are smaller and more compact, suitable for smaller kitchens or foodservice establishments. Both types offer efficient and consistent cooking results, making them popular choices in the food industry.</p></p>
