<p><p>The ceramic capacitor market consists of various types, including multilayer ceramic chip capacitors (MLCC), ceramic disc capacitors, feedthrough ceramic capacitors, and ceramic power capacitors. MLCCs are widely used in electronic devices due to their small size and high capacitance. Ceramic disc capacitors, on the other hand, are used for high-voltage applications. Feedthrough ceramic capacitors are designed for applications requiring high reliability and low leakage current. Lastly, ceramic power capacitors are utilized in high-power and high-voltage applications, providing excellent thermal stability and reliability.</p></p>
