<p><p>Quality Assurance Management System (QAMS) is a software solution that helps organizations ensure the delivery of high-quality products or services. It includes various processes and functionalities to monitor, measure, and improve quality. QAMS is available in two market types: on-premise and cloud-based. On-premise QAMS is installed and hosted locally on a company's servers, providing complete control but requiring additional maintenance and infrastructure. Cloud-based QAMS, on the other hand, is hosted on remote servers and accessed through the internet, offering greater flexibility, scalability, and cost-effectiveness as the software is managed by the vendor.</p></p>
