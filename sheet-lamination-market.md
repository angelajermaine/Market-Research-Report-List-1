<p><strong>The Sheet Lamination Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Ultrasonic Additive Manufacturing (UAM)</li><li>Laminated Object Manufacturing (LOM)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Sheet lamination is a market in which different types of technologies are used for creating objects through layering sheets of material. Ultrasonic Additive Manufacturing (UAM) is one such technology that utilizes ultrasonic vibrations to bond layers of metal sheets together. On the other hand, Laminated Object Manufacturing (LOM) involves bonding layers of paper, plastic, or metal sheets together using adhesive or heat. Both UAM and LOM provide cost-effective and efficient solutions for creating complex objects, with UAM being more suitable for metal-based products and LOM for various materials.</p></p>
