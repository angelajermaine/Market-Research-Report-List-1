<p><ul><li>High Voltage</li><li>Medium Voltage</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The power transmission cables market is divided into two types based on voltage: high voltage and medium voltage. High voltage cables are used for transmitting electricity over long distances and are typically above 66 kilovolts. They are designed to carry heavy electrical loads efficiently and have better insulation and shielding. On the other hand, medium voltage cables are used for distributing electricity within a smaller area like residential or commercial buildings. They usually operate below 66 kilovolts and have lower insulation requirements compared to high voltage cables.</p></p>
