<p><p>Manual directional control valves are used to control the flow of fluids or gases in a system. The market for manual directional control valves can be divided into various types based on the number of ways the valves control the flow. These types include 2-way, 4-way, 5-way, 6-way, and multi-way valves. </p><p>A 2-way valve has two ports, allowing for simple on/off control of fluid or gas flow. A 4-way valve has four ports, enabling the control of both the direction and the start/stop of flow. A 5-way valve is similar to a 4-way valve but includes an additional exhaust port. A 6-way valve has six ports and offers even more complex control options. Lastly, multi-way valves have more than six ports, allowing for greater flexibility and customization in controlling flow direction.</p></p>
