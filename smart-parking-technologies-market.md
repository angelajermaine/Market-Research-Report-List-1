<p><p>Smart parking technologies utilize various types of sensors to enhance parking management and provide real-time information to users. </p><p>- Ultrasonic sensors use sound waves to detect the presence of vehicles and calculate distance, allowing precise parking space monitoring. </p><p>- Radar sensors utilize radio waves to measure the distance and velocity of objects in their vicinity, enabling accurate detection and tracking of vehicles in parking spaces. </p><p>- Image sensors capture images and use image processing algorithms to recognize and analyze parking spaces, providing information about their occupancy status. These technologies play a crucial role in optimizing parking space utilization and improving overall parking management efficiency.</p></p>
