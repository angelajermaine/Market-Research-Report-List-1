<p><ul><li>Semi-Active Suspension System</li><li>Active Suspension System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The advanced suspension control market is divided into two types: semi-active suspension system and active suspension system. </p><p>Semi-active suspension system dynamically adjusts the suspension characteristics based on the road conditions and driver inputs. It provides a balance between comfort and performance by selectively controlling the damping levels.</p><p>On the other hand, the active suspension system actively controls the suspension characteristics by using sensors and actuators to continuously monitor and adjust the suspension response. It offers superior ride quality, handling, and stability, as it can quickly adapt to changing road conditions and vehicle dynamics.</p></p>
