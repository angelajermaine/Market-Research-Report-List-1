<p><p>The automatic floor scraping machine market offers three types of machines: walk-behind machines, ride-on machines, and self-propelled machines. Walk-behind machines are operated manually and typically used for smaller projects. Ride-on machines are larger and allow the operator to sit while driving and scraping. They are suitable for larger areas and provide additional comfort and efficiency. Self-propelled machines offer the convenience of automatic movement and scraping without the need for manual control. These machines are ideal for medium-sized to larger projects requiring increased productivity.</p></p>
