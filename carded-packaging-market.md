<p><p>Carded packaging refers to a type of packaging where a product is placed onto a paperboard backing and then covered with a transparent plastic blister. Thermoforming in carded packaging involves heating a plastic sheet and molding it into the desired shape before sealing it onto the paperboard backing. Cold forming, on the other hand, uses a preformed aluminum foil to create the blister pack. Both methods provide secure and visually appealing packaging solutions for various consumer goods. </p></p>
