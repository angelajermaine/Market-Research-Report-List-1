<p><p>A Silicon Controlled Rectifier (SCR) Power Controller is an electronic device that is used for controlling the amount of power supplied to loads such as heaters, motors, and lamps. It is commonly used for applications that require accurate control over power levels and where resistance-based controllers are not suitable.</p><p>The SCR power controller works by using the principle of silicon-controlled rectification, which involves switching the power supply to the load on and off at a high frequency. This allows for precise control over the average power delivered to the load. The SCR power controller can be used in both single-phase and three-phase applications, making it suitable for a wide range of industries and applications.</p><p>The market for SCR power controllers is expected to grow at a compound annual growth rate (CAGR) of 8.6% during the forecast period.
