<p><p>The pallet conveyor market consists of different types such as drag chain, roller (driven or gravity), plastic type, and slat type conveyors. Drag chain conveyors utilize a chain driven mechanism to move pallets along the conveyor. Roller conveyors come in two forms, driven and gravity, where the former is powered by a motor and the latter relies on gravity for movement. Plastic and slat type conveyors use plastic or metal slats respectively to transport pallets. Each type varies in its design, mechanism, and suitability for specific applications within the pallet conveyor market.</p></p>
