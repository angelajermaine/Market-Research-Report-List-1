<p><p>The single-layered photovoltaic curtain wall is a type of photoelectric curtain wall that consists of a single layer of photovoltaic cells, which convert sunlight into electricity. It is commonly used in buildings to generate renewable energy while providing aesthetic appeal. On the other hand, the double-layered photoelectric curtain wall is a more advanced version that includes an additional layer of insulation between the photovoltaic cells. This design enhances energy efficiency and provides better insulation for the building, reducing heating and cooling costs.</p></p>
