<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>Threaded flanges are a type of flange used in piping systems to connect pipes and valves. They consist of a flange with internal or external threading. Internal thread flanges have threads on the inside, allowing them to be screwed onto pipes or fittings with external threads. On the other hand, external thread flanges have threads on the outside, allowing them to be attached to pipes or fittings with internal threads. Both types of threaded flanges provide a secure and leak-proof connection in various industrial applications.</p></p>
