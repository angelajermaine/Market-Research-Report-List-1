<p><strong>The Biodegradable Cutlery Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>

<p><p>The biodegradable cutlery market consists of various types, including PLA, CPLA, and Starch Blends. PLA (Polylactic Acid) is a renewable, plant-based material derived from corn starch or sugarcane. It offers good heat resistance and is widely used for disposable utensils. CPLA (Crystallized Polylactic Acid) is an improved version of PLA, offering higher heat resistance and increased strength. Starch Blends are made by combining starch with other biodegradable materials. They provide good heat resistance, durability, and are suitable for food handling. These alternatives aim to reduce plastic waste and promote environmental sustainability.</p></p>
