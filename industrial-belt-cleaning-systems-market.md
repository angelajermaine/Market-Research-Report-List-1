<p><ul><li>Primary Belt Cleaner</li><li>Secondary Belt Cleaner</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The industrial belt cleaning systems market includes various types of belt cleaners used for cleaning conveyor belts in industries. The primary belt cleaner is the main cleaning device installed at the head pulley to remove bulk material and prevent it from reaching further along the conveyor. The secondary belt cleaner is positioned just after the primary cleaner to achieve more targeted and efficient cleaning. The "Others" category includes additional belt cleaning solutions like air knife cleaners, brush cleaners, and water spray systems that can be customized based on specific industry needs.</p></p>
