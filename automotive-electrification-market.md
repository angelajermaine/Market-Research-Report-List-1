Plug-in Hybrid Electric Vehicles (PHEV) have a larger battery that can be charged from an external source, offering longer electric-only range than HEVs. Battery Electric Vehicles (BEV) entirely run on electricity and need to be charged from an external source, with no reliance on gasoline or diesel.</p></p>
