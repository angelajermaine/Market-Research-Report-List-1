<p><p>The electrostatic dehydrator market can be categorized into three types: AC Field Electrostatic Dehydrator, Combined AC-DC Field Electrostatic Dehydrator, and DC Field Electrostatic Dehydrator. </p><p>In an AC Field Electrostatic Dehydrator, the process of dehydration is achieved using an alternating electric field. The Combined AC-DC Field Electrostatic Dehydrator utilizes both alternating and direct electric fields to remove moisture from substances. Lastly, the DC Field Electrostatic Dehydrator solely relies on a direct electric field to accomplish dehydration. These various market types provide different options for industries to choose from based on their specific dehydration requirements.</p></p>
