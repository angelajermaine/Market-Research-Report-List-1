<p><p>The cement and mortar testing equipment market includes various types of machines used for testing the quality and properties of cement and mortar. Compression machines are used to assess the strength and durability of cement samples. Humidity cabinets simulate different environmental conditions to check the moisture resistance of cement and mortar. Tensile testing machines measure the ability of cement to withstand tension. Blaine apparatus is used to determine the fineness of cement particles. Cement autoclaves assess the effects of steam on cement samples. Bond strength testers evaluate the adhesion of mortar to different surfaces.</p></p>
