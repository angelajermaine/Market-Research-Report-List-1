<p><p>Distribution Automation Controllers (DAC) are devices used to monitor and control electrical distribution systems. The market for DAC can be categorized into two types: distributed type and centralized type. In the distributed type market, the controllers are installed at various points in the distribution network, allowing for localized control and quick reaction to faults or disruptions. On the other hand, the centralized type market involves a central controller that oversees the entire distribution system, enabling coordinated decision-making and optimized operation. Both types aim to enhance reliability, efficiency, and flexibility in distribution systems.</p></p>
