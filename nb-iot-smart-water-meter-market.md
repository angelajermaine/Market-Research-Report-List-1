<p><strong>The NB-IoT Smart Water Meter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Dry Type</li><li>Wet Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>NB-IoT Smart Water Meters are classified into two types: Dry Type and Wet Type. The Dry Type meter is installed outside the water pipe, using ultrasonic sensors to measure water flow without direct contact. It is suitable for locations where water pressure is not high. Wet Type meters, on the other hand, are installed inside the water pipe. They use a mechanical design to measure water flow and are suitable for locations with high water pressure. Both types enable real-time monitoring and data transmission through NB-IoT technology, providing efficient water management solutions.</p></p>
