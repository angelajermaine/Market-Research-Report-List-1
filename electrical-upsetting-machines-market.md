<p><p>Electrical upsetting machines are industrial machines used to forge or upset the ends of metal rods, bars, or tubes. This technology is widely used in various industries such as automotive, aerospace, construction, and metalworking, to create strong and durable joints in metal components.</p><p>The global market for electrical upsetting machines is currently experiencing steady growth and is projected to continue growing in the coming years. The market is primarily driven by the increasing demand for advanced and reliable metal fabrication techniques.
