<p><strong>The In Display Fingerprint Scanner Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Optical</li><li>Ultrasonic</li><li>Capacitive</li></ul></p>
<p><p>In the display fingerprint scanner market, there are three different types: optical, ultrasonic, and capacitive. Optical scanners use light to capture an image of the fingerprint, which is then used for authentication. Ultrasonic scanners use sound waves to create a 3D image of the fingerprint, offering higher security and accuracy. Capacitive scanners detect the electrical current produced by the ridges in the fingerprint, providing a reliable and fast authentication method. Each type has its own advantages and capabilities, catering to different user preferences and security needs.</p></p>
