<p><p>Dental charting software is available in two main types: On-Premise and Cloud-Based. On-Premise software is installed locally on a dental clinic's computers and servers, allowing complete control over data storage and security. However, it requires maintenance and updates by the clinic's staff. In contrast, Cloud-Based software operates on remote servers accessible through the internet. It offers greater mobility, as dentists can access patient records anytime from any device. While data security is maintained by the software provider, internet connectivity is crucial for its usage.</p></p>
