<p><p>Broadloom carpet refers to a type of carpet that is woven on a wide loom, resulting in a seamless and wall-to-wall floor covering. There are three main market types within broadloom carpets: cut pile, loop pile, and cut and loop pile. Cut pile carpets have their loops cut, resulting in a plush and velvety appearance. Loop pile carpets, on the other hand, retain the loops, creating a textured and durable surface. Cut and loop pile carpets blend both cut and looped fibers, providing a mix of smooth and textured areas for a unique and versatile look.</p></p>
