<p><p>Orthopedic Radiology Equipment market consists of three major types: X-ray equipment, CT equipment, and MRI equipment. X-ray equipment utilizes electromagnetic radiation to create images of bones and joints, helping diagnose fractures and other skeletal abnormalities. CT equipment combines X-ray technology with computer processing to generate detailed cross-sectional images, enhancing the detection of fractures and musculoskeletal disorders. MRI equipment uses powerful magnets and radio waves to produce high-resolution images, providing detailed information on soft tissues, ligaments, and tendons. These equipment types play a crucial role in diagnosing and monitoring orthopedic conditions.</p></p>
