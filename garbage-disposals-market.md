<p><p>Garbage disposals are kitchen appliances that shred food waste into small particles, allowing them to be easily washed away. There are two types of garbage disposal systems in the market. Batch feed disposals require users to load the waste and activate the unit with a stopper. This type is generally considered safer but slower. On the other hand, continuous feed disposals operate by turning on the unit while waste is being added. Although faster, they are less safe as they can be operated with the cover open.</p></p>
