<p><p>Ground handling services refer to the various support and management services provided to an aircraft during its time on the ground, both before and after a flight. These services include aircraft loading and unloading, passenger and baggage handling, aircraft cleaning, fuelling, catering, maintenance and repair, and coordination with other airport services. Ground handling services play a crucial role in ensuring a smooth and efficient turnaround of aircraft, as well as enhancing the overall passenger experience.</p><p>The ground handling services market has been experiencing significant growth in recent years and is expected to continue expanding at a CAGR of 14.4% during the forecast period. Several factors are driving the growth of this market. Firstly, the increasing demand for air travel, particularly in emerging economies, has led to a rise in aircraft movements, thereby boosting the demand for ground handling services. Secondly, airports and airlines are increasingly outsourcing their ground handling operations to specialized service providers in order to focus on their core competencies and reduce costs. This outsourcing trend is further contributing to the growth of the market. Moreover, the introduction of advanced technologies and automation in ground handling services is enhancing operational efficiency, reducing turnaround times, and improving safety and security standards.</p><p>In terms of trends, there is a growing focus on sustainability and eco-friendly practices in the ground handling services market. Market players are adopting energy-efficient technologies, using electric ground support equipment, and implementing waste management and recycling initiatives to minimize their environmental impact. Additionally, the increasing adoption of digital solutions such as mobile apps, self-check-in kiosks, and automated boarding gates is revolutionizing the passenger experience and streamlining ground handling operations. Overall, the ground handling services market is set to witness substantial growth in the coming years, driven by increasing air traffic and the adoption of advanced technologies and sustainable practices.</p></p>
