<p><strong>The Medical Exoskeleton Robots Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Lower</li><li>Upper</li><li>Full Body</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Medical exoskeleton robots can be categorized into three types based on the level of support they provide: Lower body, upper body, and full body exoskeletons. Lower body exoskeletons assist with walking movements and provide support to the lower limbs, making it easier for individuals with mobility impairments to walk. Upper body exoskeletons focus on aiding arm movements and enhancing upper body strength. Full body exoskeletons offer support to both upper and lower limbs, providing comprehensive assistance for individuals with more severe mobility limitations.</p></p>
