<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Air Filtration Media Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Spunbond</li><li>Needlefelt</li><li>Wet laid</li><li>Melt blown</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Air filtration media market comprises of different types such as spunbond, needlefelt, wet laid, and melt blown. Spunbond media is made by spinning and bonding fibers together, offering high strength and durability. Needlefelt media involves punching fibers into a felt-like material, providing high filtration efficiency. Wet laid media is created by suspending fibers in water and forming a sheet, offering customizable properties. Melt blown media is produced by melting polymer resins and blowing them onto a collector, providing excellent filtration capabilities. These different types cater to various filtration needs and industries.</p></p>
