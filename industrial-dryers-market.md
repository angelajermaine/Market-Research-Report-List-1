<p><p>Industrial dryers are used in various industries to remove moisture from a given material or product. There are three main types of industrial dryers: direct dryers, indirect dryers, and specialty dryers. Direct dryers use direct contact between the material and hot gases to remove moisture. Indirect dryers, on the other hand, use heated surfaces to transfer heat to the material. Specialty dryers are designed for specific applications, such as rotary dryers for drying bulk solid materials or fluidized bed dryers for drying particulate materials by suspending them in a high-velocity stream of hot air.</p></p>
