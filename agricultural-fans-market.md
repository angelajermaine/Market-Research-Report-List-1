<p><ul><li>Centrifugal Fans</li><li>Axial Fans</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Agricultural fans are used in various applications in the farming industry. Centrifugal fans are commonly used for ventilation and air circulation in barns, poultry houses, and greenhouses. They are designed to move air in a straight line using spinning blades. Axial fans, on the other hand, are more suitable for larger spaces such as grain bins and silos. They are characterized by their propeller-like blades that move air parallel to the fan shaft. Both types of fans play an essential role in maintaining optimal conditions for agriculture.</p></p>
