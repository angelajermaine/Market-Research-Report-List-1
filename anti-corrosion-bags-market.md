<p><p>The Anti-Corrosion Bags market is categorized into two main types: Mono-Layer and Multi-Layer. Mono-Layer bags are made from a single layer of material, providing a basic level of protection against corrosion. On the other hand, Multi-Layer bags consist of multiple layers, each serving a specific purpose such as barrier protection, moisture resistance, or temperature regulation. These bags are designed to offer superior corrosion prevention by combining different materials with unique properties. Both types aim to safeguard products from corrosion, but multi-layer bags provide enhanced protection due to their advanced construction.</p></p>
