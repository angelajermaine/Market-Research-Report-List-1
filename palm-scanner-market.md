<p><p>Palm scanner markets can be categorized into two types: optical scanning and vein scanning. Optical scanning refers to the use of advanced sensors to capture palm prints, fingerprints, or hand geometry for identification purposes. It mainly focuses on the surface patterns and characteristics of the palm. On the other hand, vein scanning utilizes near-infrared light to capture the unique vein patterns beneath the skin's surface. This technology offers a higher level of security and accuracy as vein patterns are extremely difficult to replicate, ensuring reliable authentication.</p></p>
