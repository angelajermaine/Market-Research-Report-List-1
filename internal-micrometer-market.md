<p><p>An internal micrometer, also known as a bore gauge or internal bore micrometer, is a measuring tool used to accurately measure the inside diameter of a hole or cylindrical object. <p><p>The internal micrometer market consists of two main types: digital internal micrometers and analogue internal micrometers. Digital internal micrometers have a digital display that provides precise measurements and eliminates the need for manual reading. They are commonly used in industries where accuracy is crucial. Both types serve the purpose of measuring the inside dimensions of objects but differ in terms of accuracy and ease of use.</p></p>
