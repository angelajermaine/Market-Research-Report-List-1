<p><p>Thermal overload relays are devices used to protect electrical motors from overheating. On the other hand, automatic reset thermal overload relays automatically reset after a cooling period, allowing for easy and convenient operation. Both types of relays play a crucial role in maintaining motor safety and preventing damage caused by excessive heat.</p></p>
