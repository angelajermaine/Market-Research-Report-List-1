<p><p>Rapid prototyping equipment refers to the tools used to create physical models or prototypes of products quickly and accurately. The market types in this sector include Stereolithography Apparatus (SLA), Three Dimension Printing (3DP), Selective Laser Sintering (SLS), Laminated Object Manufacturing (LOM), Fused Deposition Modeling (FDM), and others. SLA uses lasers to solidify liquid resin, 3DP builds objects layer by layer from powder materials, SLS employs lasers to fuse powdered materials, LOM cuts and glues paper or plastic sheets together, FDM extrudes molten plastic layer by layer, while other types encompass various technologies like Digital Light Processing (DLP) or Electron Beam Melting (EBM).</p></p>
