<p><p>Carpet tapes are classified into two main types: Tackified Acrylic Adhesive and Natural Rubber Adhesive. Tackified Acrylic Adhesive carpet tapes have an adhesive made with tackifiers and acrylic polymers. These tapes offer high initial tack and adhesive strength, making them suitable for a variety of surfaces. On the other hand, Natural Rubber Adhesive carpet tapes are made with natural rubber, providing excellent adhesion and flexibility. These tapes are mainly used for heavyweight carpets or where extra durability is required. Both types of carpet tapes serve as reliable adhesives for securing carpets and rugs to different flooring surfaces.</p></p>
