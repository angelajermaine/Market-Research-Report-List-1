<p><p>The installation vessels market can be broadly categorized into two types based on their operational capabilities: shallow sea and deep sea vessels. Shallow sea vessels are designed to operate in relatively shallow water depths, typically up to 200 meters. They are suitable for offshore activities like wind farm installations, pipe-laying, and cable laying. On the other hand, deep sea vessels are capable of working in greater water depths, exceeding 200 meters. These vessels are often utilized for complex operations such as deep-water drilling, subsea construction, and offshore oil and gas projects.</p></p>
