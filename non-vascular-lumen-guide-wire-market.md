<p><ul><li>Straight Guidewire</li><li>Elbow Guidewire</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The non-vascular lumen guide wire market consists of two types: straight guidewires and elbow guidewires. Straight guidewires are used in procedures requiring a straight access to the anatomical site, as they have a straight tip. On the other hand, elbow guidewires have a curved tip and are used in procedures where access needs to be made around bends or corners in the vasculature. Both types of guidewires assist in navigating and positioning medical devices during non-vascular procedures.</p></p>
