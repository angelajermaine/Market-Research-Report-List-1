<p><p>The Social Content Management System (CMS) market is categorized into two main types: Cloud-Based and On-Premise solutions. Cloud-Based CMS refers to a system hosted on remote servers, allowing users to access it via the internet and eliminating the need for local infrastructure and maintenance. On the other hand, On-Premise CMS involves installing the software on local servers within an organization's premises and managing it internally. These two options offer different deployment models based on the user's preference and requirements, providing flexibility and scalability options for managing social content effectively.</p></p>
