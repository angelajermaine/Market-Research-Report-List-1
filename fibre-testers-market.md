<p><p>Fibre testers are used to evaluate various properties of fibers or fabrics. Tensile testers measure the strength and elasticity of fibres by applying tension. Abrasion testers assess the durability of textiles under rubbing and wearing conditions. Color fastness testers determine the color resistance to fading or bleeding. Air permeability testers measure the breathability of fabrics. Waterproofness testers assess the resistance of materials to water penetration. Other types of fibre testers may include devices for testing shrinkage, pilling, flammability, or dimensional stability among others. These testers help ensure the quality and performance of textiles in various industries.</p></p>
