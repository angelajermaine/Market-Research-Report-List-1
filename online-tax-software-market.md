<p><p>The online tax software market can be categorized into two types: cloud-based and on-premises. Cloud-based tax software is hosted on remote servers and accessed via the internet, providing scalability and flexibility to users. It allows multiple users to work concurrently and offers automatic updates and data backup. On the other hand, on-premises tax software is installed locally on a user's computer or server. It offers more control and customization options but requires manual updates and maintenance. Both types aim to assist users in managing and filing their taxes efficiently.</p></p>
