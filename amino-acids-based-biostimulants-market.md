<p><p>Amino acids based biostimulants market can be categorized into three types: foliar treatment, soil treatment, and seed treatment. Foliar treatment involves applying biostimulants directly on the leaves of plants, which are then absorbed through the leaf surface. Soil treatment involves incorporating biostimulants into the soil, either by spraying or mixing in. Seed treatment involves coating the seeds with biostimulants before planting. Each type of treatment provides different benefits and targets specific plant growth stages, allowing farmers to enhance crop productivity, improve nutrient uptake, and strengthen plant resistance against various stress factors.</p></p>
