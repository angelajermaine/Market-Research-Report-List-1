<p><p>A percutaneous thrombectomy device is a medical device used to remove blood clots from arteries and veins. It is a minimally invasive procedure that involves inserting a catheter with a clot retrieval device into the blood vessel to physically remove the clot.</p><p>The market for percutaneous thrombectomy devices is experiencing significant growth due to the rising prevalence of cardiovascular diseases such as deep vein thrombosis and pulmonary embolism. Moreover, the growing geriatric population and increasing adoption of minimally invasive procedures are also contributing to market growth. The percutaneous thrombectomy device market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 7.9% during the forecast period.</p><p>One of the latest trends in the percutaneous thrombectomy device market is the development of advanced catheters and clot retrieval devices. Manufacturers are focusing on enhancing the efficiency and effectiveness of these devices to provide better patient outcomes. For instance, some devices incorporate advanced imaging technologies to improve navigation and visualization during the procedure.</p><p>Another trend in the market is the increasing adoption of mechanical thrombectomy devices. These devices use mechanical force to remove blood clots and have shown promising results in terms of clot removal and patient outcomes. They are being widely used in the treatment of acute ischemic stroke.</p><p>Furthermore, the growing investment in healthcare infrastructure and increasing healthcare expenditure in developing countries are creating opportunities for market growth. These factors, coupled with advancements in technology and the rising demand for minimally invasive procedures, are expected to drive the growth of the percutaneous thrombectomy device market in the coming years.</p></p>
