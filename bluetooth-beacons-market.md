<p><p>Bluetooth beacons are devices that use Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE) technology to transmit signals to nearby smartphones. There are three major market types: iBeacon, Eddystone, and Microsoft 10 market. iBeacon is Apple's proprietary beacon format primarily used within iOS devices. Eddystone, developed by Google, is an open-source standard compatible with both iOS and Android. Microsoft 10 market refers to beacons that are compatible with devices running Microsoft's Windows 10 operating system. These market types enable businesses to deliver location-based, personalized experiences and engage with customers through targeted notifications on their mobile devices.</p></p>
