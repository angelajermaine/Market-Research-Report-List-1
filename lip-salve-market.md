<p><p>The lip salve market is divided into two main types: solid cream type and liquid gel type. They come in a solid form that needs to be applied with fingers or a lip brush. On the other hand, liquid gel type lip salves have a more gel-like consistency that is easy to apply directly from a tube or rollerball. They often contain hydrating oils or extracts to provide instant relief and hydration to the lips.</p></p>
