<p><p>The Scientific CMOS (sCMOS) Camera market consists of two main types: Front Illuminated and Back Illuminated. Front Illuminated cameras use a micro lens array to direct light onto the sensor, allowing for efficient light collection. This type is commonly used in applications that require high resolution and sensitivity. On the other hand, Back Illuminated cameras have a back-thinned sensor, which allows light to directly reach the sensor without any obstructions. This type is suitable for applications where maximum sensitivity is crucial, such as low-light imaging and microscopy.</p></p>
