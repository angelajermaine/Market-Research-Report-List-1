<p><p>Pneumatic surgical drills are categorized into two main types: wired type drills and wireless type drills. Wired type drills are connected to a power source via a cord, providing a reliable and continuous power supply during surgeries. On the other hand, wireless type drills operate using a rechargeable battery, offering greater flexibility and ease of movement for surgeons. Both types of drills are essential tools in the medical field, and the choice between wired and wireless depends on the specific needs of the surgical procedure.</p></p>
