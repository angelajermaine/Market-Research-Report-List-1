<p><p>Taps and dies are tools commonly used in machining and manufacturing processes to create threaded holes or fasteners. Taps are used to create internal threads in holes, whereas dies are used to create external threads on rods or bolts. They are critical tools in industries such as automotive, aerospace, construction, and machinery.</p><p>The global taps and dies market is expected to experience significant growth in the forecast period. Factors driving this growth include the increasing demand for taps and dies in manufacturing processes to produce high-quality threaded components.
