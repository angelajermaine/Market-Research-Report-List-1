<p><p>The Lower Limb Exoskeleton market consists of two main types: Powered and Passive. Powered exoskeletons are designed to provide assistance and augmentation to the lower limbs through the use of electric motors or actuators. These devices offer enhanced mobility and strength to individuals with lower limb impairments. On the other hand, passive exoskeletons do not have any powered assistance and rely on mechanical structures and springs to provide support and assistance to the lower limbs. These devices are typically lighter and more affordable, making them suitable for individuals with mild mobility issues.</p></p>
