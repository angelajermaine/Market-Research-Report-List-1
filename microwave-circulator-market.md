<p><p>The microwave circulator market can be categorized into three types: passive, three-port, and four-port. A passive circulator allows the flow of signals in a single direction, while blocking them in the opposite direction. A three-port circulator enables the simultaneous flow of signals from one port to the other two ports, while isolating each port from one another. Similarly, a four-port circulator facilitates the flow of signals between all four ports, while ensuring isolation between them. These different types cater to diverse applications and requirements within the microwave industry.</p></p>
