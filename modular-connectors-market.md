<p><p>Modular connectors are widely used in various industries for connecting different devices. There are different types of modular connectors available in the market. The 4P4C connectors consist of four positions and four conductors, commonly used for telephone handsets. The 6P6C connectors have six positions and six conductors, commonly used for Ethernet connections. The 8P8C connectors, also known as RJ45 connectors, are widely used for Ethernet networking and telecommunications. The 10P10C connectors have ten positions and ten conductors, commonly used for computer networking and telephony applications.</p></p>
