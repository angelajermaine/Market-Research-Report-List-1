<p><ul><li>Automatic Fire Extinguishing System</li><li>Non-automatic Fire Extinguishing System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The fire extinguishing system market comprises two main types: automatic fire extinguishing systems and non-automatic fire extinguishing systems. Automatic fire extinguishing systems are designed to detect and automatically suppress fires without human intervention. They typically have sensors and actuators that detect heat, smoke, or flames and initiate fire suppression methods like sprinklers or gas flooding systems. Non-automatic fire extinguishing systems, on the other hand, require human intervention to manually operate and deploy fire suppression techniques like portable fire extinguishers or hose reels.</p></p>
