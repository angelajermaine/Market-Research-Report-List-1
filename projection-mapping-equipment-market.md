<p><p>Projection mapping equipment market can be classified into two major types: Digital Light Processing (DLP) and Liquid Crystal Display (LCD). DLP technology utilizes micro mirrors to reflect light, resulting in high contrast, sharp images with excellent color accuracy and a smooth visual experience. On the other hand, LCD technology uses liquid crystals to control light transmission, offering vibrant colors and high brightness levels. Both types play a crucial role in the projection mapping industry, providing users with versatile and high-quality projection solutions for various applications.</p></p>
