<p><p>The braiding machine market consists of two main types: vertical braiders and horizontal braiders. Vertical braiders are machines that have a vertical setup where the carrier rotates around a vertical axis. They are ideal for producing tubular, flexible, and round braids. On the other hand, horizontal braiders have a horizontal setup where the carrier rotates around a horizontal axis. They are commonly used for producing flat, wide, and dense braids. Both types of machines are widely used in industries such as textiles, automotive, and aerospace for various braiding applications.</p></p>
