<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The parts washing cabinet market is divided into two main types: solvent-based and aqueous-based. Solvent-based parts washing cabinets use solvents, such as mineral spirits or hydrocarbon-based solutions, to remove contaminants from parts. These cabinets are ideal for heavy-duty cleaning and can effectively dissolve oil, grease, and other stubborn residues. On the other hand, aqueous-based parts washing cabinets utilize water-based detergents or cleaners for the cleaning process. These cabinets are more environmentally friendly and typically require lower operating costs compared to solvent-based ones.</p></p>
