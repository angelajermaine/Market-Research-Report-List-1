<p><strong>The Heavy Rotary Drilling Rig Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Crawler</li><li>Rubber-Tired</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Heavy Rotary Drilling Rig market can be categorized into two main types: Crawler and Rubber-Tired. Crawler drilling rigs are equipped with caterpillar tracks, providing them with excellent mobility and maneuverability on rough terrains. They are ideal for projects that require drilling in challenging locations. On the other hand, Rubber-Tired drilling rigs are mounted on rubber tires, making them more suitable for softer terrains and urban areas. They offer increased mobility and easier transportation, making them a preferred choice for projects that involve drilling in multiple locations.</p></p>
