<p><p>An Atomic Fluorescence Spectrometer (AFS) is an analytical instrument used to measure the concentration of elements in a sample. AFS is widely used in various industries such as environmental monitoring, pharmaceuticals, food and beverage, and mining, among others.</p><p>The Atomic Fluorescence Spectrometer (AFS) market has been witnessing steady growth in recent years.
