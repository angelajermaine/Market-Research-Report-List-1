<p><p>Residential Fuel Cell market is divided into two major types: PEM and SOFC. PEM (Proton Exchange Membrane) fuel cells use a polymer membrane to conduct protons, requiring pure hydrogen as fuel, making them more suitable for residential applications due to their compact size and fast start-up. On the other hand, SOFC (Solid Oxide Fuel Cell) market is based on cells operating at high temperatures, using a solid oxide electrolyte for ion transportation and enabling them to power larger houses or buildings due to their high energy conversion efficiency and ability to use various fuels.</p></p>
