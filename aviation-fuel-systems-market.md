<p><p>The aviation fuel systems market can be categorized into three types: auxiliary fuel systems, rotorcraft fuel systems, and fixed wing fuel systems. Auxiliary fuel systems refer to additional tanks that provide extra fuel storage capacity, increasing the range of an aircraft. Rotorcraft fuel systems are designed specifically for helicopters, ensuring efficient fuel supply to the engines. Fixed wing fuel systems are installed in fixed-wing aircraft, managing the storage, transfer, and delivery of fuel. These fuel systems play a crucial role in the aviation industry, enabling the safe and efficient operation of aircraft.</p></p>
