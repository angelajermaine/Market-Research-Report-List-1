<p><p>Diaphragm hydraulic accumulators are devices used to store hydraulic energy in a system. They support two main types: welded type and threaded type. Welded type accumulators are constructed with a welded diaphragm seal, ensuring high strength and reliable performance. Both types serve the same purpose of energy storage in hydraulic systems, but their structural design and connection methods differ, giving users options based on their specific requirements.</p></p>
