<p><strong>The Ultrasonic Scaler Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Magnetostrictive Ultrasonic Scaler</li><li>Piezoelectric Ultrasonic Scaler</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Ultrasonic scaler market consists of two main types: magnetostrictive and piezoelectric scalers. Magnetostrictive ultrasonic scalers use a metal stack transducer that converts electrical energy into mechanical vibrations, resulting in the scaling motion. They produce a elliptical or orbital vibration pattern. On the other hand, piezoelectric ultrasonic scalers utilize a ceramic transducer that converts electrical energy into linear motion vibrations. The vibrations are transferred to the scaling tip, creating a back and forth movement. Both types effectively remove tartar and plaque from teeth during dental cleanings.</p></p>
