<p><p>Composite bearings are a kind of bearings that are made up of two or more materials in order to enhance their performance. Fiber Matrix composite bearings incorporate fibers (such as carbon or glass) within a matrix material (like epoxy) to improve strength and stiffness. Metal Matrix composite bearings, on the other hand, replace the matrix material with metal, resulting in greater wear resistance and thermal stability. Both types of composite bearings offer unique advantages, catering to specific application requirements, such as reducing friction, increasing durability, and enhancing overall performance.</p></p>
