<p><p>Wheat and corn dryers are categorized into two market types: stationary and mobile. Stationary dryers are fixed installations that are permanently placed in a specific location, typically on the farm or in a facility. They are designed to handle large volumes of grain and are generally more expensive to purchase and maintain. On the other hand, mobile dryers are portable units that can be easily transported and utilized at various locations. They are often preferred by smaller-scale farmers or those who need flexibility in grain drying operations.</p></p>
