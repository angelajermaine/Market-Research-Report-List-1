<p><ul><li>Closed Loop</li><li>Open Loop</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Inert Gas Cryocoolers market can be categorized into two types: closed loop and open loop. A closed loop cryocooler operates in a cyclic process where a continuous flow of inert gas circulates within a closed circuit, efficiently cooling the system. On the other hand, an open loop cryocooler discharges the used gas into the environment after cooling the system. Both types have their advantages and applications, and the choice depends on factors such as efficiency, cost, and environmental impact.</p></p>
