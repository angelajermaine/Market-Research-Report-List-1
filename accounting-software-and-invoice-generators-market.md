<p><p>The accounting software market consists of two main types: on-premises and cloud-based solutions. On-premises accounting software refers to software that is installed and operated on the company's own servers and computers. It requires maintenance, updates, and backups to be managed internally. In contrast, cloud-based accounting software operates on remote servers and is accessed via the internet. Users do not need to manage infrastructure, as all data and operations are handled by the software provider. Cloud-based solutions offer flexibility, scalability, and real-time collaboration, while on-premises solutions provide greater control and security.</p></p>
