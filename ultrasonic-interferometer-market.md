<p><p>Ultrasonic interferometer is a scientific instrument used to measure the velocity of ultrasonic waves in a liquid medium. It works on the principle of interference of sound waves produced by the source and reflected from the liquid interface. By measuring the phase difference between the two waves, the velocity of sound can be calculated, which in turn helps determine various properties of the liquid, such as density, viscosity, and concentration.</p><p>The future outlook of the ultrasonic interferometer market looks promising.
