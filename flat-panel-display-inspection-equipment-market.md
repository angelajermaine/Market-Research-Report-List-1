<p><p>The Flat Panel Display inspection equipment market comprises three main types: Automatic Optical Inspection (AOI), Array Test, and Cell Test. AOI systems use cameras and image processing techniques to detect and analyze defects in flat panel displays during different stages of production. Array Test equipment performs functional testing and quality checks on large arrays of displays. Cell Test systems evaluate individual cells within a display to ensure their performance and quality. These inspection equipment types are essential for ensuring the overall quality and reliability of flat panel displays in various industries.</p></p>
